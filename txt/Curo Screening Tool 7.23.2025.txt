Curo Screening Tool

Name: ___________________________________ Date: _________________ 

1. Do you have any allergies or a severe adverse reaction to any of the following? 
a. If yes do not proceed
i.  Non-Steroidal Anti-inflammatories (NSAIDS)- ibuprofen (Advil), naproxen (Aleve), diclofenac (voltaren).
ii. Famotidine (Pepcid)
iii. Ondansetron (Zofran)
iv. Acetaminophen (Tylenol)
v. Caffeine

2. Are you currently taking NSAIDS daily?					YES or NO
a. If yes, do not proceed

3. Have you taken any NSAIDS in the last 6 hours?				YES or NO
a. If yes do not proceed

4. Do you have a any of the listed past medical history?			YES or NO
a. If Yes, do not proceed
i. Ulcers/History of Ulcers
ii. GI Bled/History of GI Bleeds
iii. Renal Failure or Kidney Disease
iv. Clotting Disorders
v. High Risk Delirium 
vi. Liver Disease
vii. Heart Arrhythmias, Heart Disease or QT prolongation
viii. Seizures
5. Do you have a current medical history of alcoholism or consume alcohol more than 3 times pwer week? 
a. If yes, do not proceed

6. Are you Pregnant or Breastfeeding?						YES or NO
a. If yes, do not proceed
7. Are you 18 years of age or older? 
a. If No, do not proceed
8. Do you currently have cancer or are on chemotherapy? 
a. If yes, do not proceed
9. Are you currently taking any of the following
a. If yes, do not proceed
i.  Anti-depressants such as SSRI's or other
ii. Seizure medications
iii. Anti-Arrhythmic
iv. Cancer therapy

10. Are you currently taking medication in the following categories?		YES or NO
a. If yes proceed to consult 
i. Anti-Inflammatories
ii. Anti-Virals, including HIV meds
iii. Immunosuppressants
iv. Loop Diuretic (Hydrochlorothiazide)
v. Anti-Coagulants (blood thinners)
vi. Anit-Hypertensives (blood pressure medications)
vii. Anti-biotics
viii. Anti-fungals 
ix. Anti-Parasitic
x. Hydroxychloroquine
xi. Gastric Motilty Agents
xii. Anti-psychotics 
xiii. Mood stabilizers 
xiv. Muscle Relaxants
xv. Pain Meds
xvi. Received the pneumonia vaccine within the last 14 days
xvii. Nicotine
