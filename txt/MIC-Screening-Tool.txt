MIC Products Screening Questions

1. Do you currently take any supplements containing Methionine, Inositol, Choline orally daily?		Yes or No
a. If yes, notify prescriber

2. Are you currently pregnant or breastfeeding? 		Yes or No
a. If Yes, Do not administer MIC containing products

3. Are you currently undergoing chemotherapy? 		Yes or No 
a. If Yes, Do not administer MIC containing products


Any Client with severe kidney, disease, liver disease, uncontrolled diabetes, on chemotherapy/radiation or presenting with complications needs a telehealth consult prior to receiving any services.
