Anti-Aging Topical Screening Tool


1. Have you ever had an adverse or allergic reaction to topical medication in the past- Hydroquinone, Hydrocortisone, Ascorbic acid, Tretinoin, Estriol or to any of its ingredients? 	YES OR NO
a. If yes- proceed to consult
2. What products have you tried previously, including over the counter products?
a. Please list and indicate what you are currently taking or using
3. Are you pregnant or breastfeeding?
a. If yes- proceed to consult, do not dispense tretinoin
4. Do you spend excess time in the sun?
a. If yes- proceed to consult- meds may increase sun sensitivity
5. Do you have a personal medical history involving any of the following medical conditions? YES or No
a. If yes, please explain and proceed to consult 
i. Skin- all products have sun sensitivity and side effects on skin, including but not limited to drying, itching, redness, irritation
ii. Eczema- caution tretinoin use 
6. Please provide any information on why you want to try an anti-aging topical or what led you to this service? Examples include: skin discoloration, wrinkles, better skin care and aging
